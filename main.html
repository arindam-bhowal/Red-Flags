Our Inspritation

Staying in Northeast India, and having seen how climate change has affected our communities. The seasonal floods triggered by extreme rainfall have ravaged the state. Seeing the opportunity to help prevent this fate, we were quickly sold on the necessity of the project. We noticed that the Swiss government had their own portal for displaying climate data and thus challenged ourselves to simplify the data they had for the common man.
